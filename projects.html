FeelsLike

FeelsLike is a "better weather" app that really helps you get to know your new city via sights, sounds, tastes, smells, and how it feels. Compare what the weather feels like compared to your hometown through interactive graphs built with D3.js.

Reech

Reech is the app for helping friends. Think of it as a personal quora for getting solutions to everyday problems from your friends and your extended network.
